Our Story

Welcome to Hound Dog Hills, where tradition meets adventure in the heart of Minnesota. For over 25 years, we've been creating unforgettable outdoor experiences for hunting enthusiasts, dog lovers, and nature seekers alike. Nestled in the rolling hills near Dunvilla, our 1,500-acre preserve offers pristine woodlands, private lakes, and vast open fields - the perfect backdrop for your next great adventure.

The Beginning
