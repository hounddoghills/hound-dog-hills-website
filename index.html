Our Story

A Legacy of the Outdoors

Welcome to Hound Dog Hills, where tradition meets adventure in the heart of Minnesota. For over 25 years, we've been creating unforgettable outdoor experiences for hunting enthusiasts, dog lovers, and nature seekers alike.

Nestled in the rolling hills near Dunvilla, our 1,500-acre preserve offers pristine woodlands, private lakes, and vast open fields - the perfect backdrop for your next great adventure.

Our Journey

The Hound Dog Story

1998

The Beginning

Founded with a vision to create Minnesota's premier hunting and outdoor experience. Started with just 200 acres and a dream.

2005

Dog Training Center

Expanded to include professional dog training facilities. Added obedience programs and field training for hunting companions.

2012

Cabin Lodging

Opened luxury cabin accommodations, allowing guests to extended their stays and experience the full Minnesota wilderness.

2024

Today

Now spanning 1,500 acres with 3 private lakes, we continue to deliver world-class outdoor experiences for generations of families.
